How to enjoy

aburasoba
  1. When it's hot, pour vinegar and chili oil like drawing circles.Two circles of each for Regular size. Three circles of each for Large size.
  2. Mix it well right away including the noodles and the sauce in the bottom.
  3. Eat it while it's hot. Add chopped onions on the table or an extra soft-boiled egg(Â¥100) for more velvety as you like.

tsukemen
  1. choose the amount of the noodles from regular, medium, and large(It's free of charge in spite of the sizes).
  2. Add extra toppings, such as eggs, and create your own tsukemen.
  3. Add free chopped onions, shaved flakes, and vinegar on the table as you like.
  4. After finishing noodles, order "Soup-Wari"(free) and add into the lest of the soup, so that you can finish and still enjoy the whole soup in the bowl.
